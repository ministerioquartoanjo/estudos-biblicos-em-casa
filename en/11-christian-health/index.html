The 8 Natural Remedies

God's gifts for a healthy and balanced life

"Pure air, sunlight, abstemiousness, rest, exercise, proper diet, the use of water, trust in Divine power—these are the true remedies."

— The Ministry of Healing, p. 127

Rest

Adequate rest is essential for physical and mental recovery. God established the Sabbath as a weekly day of rest for our benefit.

Pure Water

Water is essential for all bodily functions. Drinking pure water in adequate amounts helps detoxify the body and maintain cell health.

Fresh Air

Breathing fresh air improves blood oxygenation and strengthens the immune system. Nature offers us pure and renewed air.

Sunlight

Moderate exposure to sunlight is vital for vitamin D production, improves mood and strengthens the immune system.

Physical Exercise

Regular physical activity strengthens the heart, improves circulation, increases energy and helps maintain a healthy weight.

Healthy Diet

A diet based on whole foods, fruits, vegetables, legumes, grains and nuts provides all the nutrients needed for optimal health.

Temperance

Balance in all good things and abstinence from what is harmful. Temperance helps us avoid excesses and addictions.

Trust in God

Faith in God and trust in His care are essential for spiritual, emotional, and physical health. Prayer and Bible study strengthen our connection with Him.
