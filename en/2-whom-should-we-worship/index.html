Introduction

If you ask someone to bring you a glass of water and they bring you a bucket full of sand, are you satisfied? No. Likewise, God is not satisfied when we present ourselves before Him to worship, and we do not do it the way He asked.

Men have become accustomed to offering what they think God will accept. But He has clearly explained in His Word how He wishes to be worshipped. We will learn that today.

Who is God?

The Only True God - The Father

The Bible clearly teaches that there is only one God - the Father. He is the Creator of all things, the only one who deserves our supreme worship.

"But to us there is but one God, the Father, of whom are all things, and we in him..."

1 Corinthians 8:6

"Hear, O Israel: The LORD our God is one LORD."

Deuteronomy 6:4

Who is Jesus?

The Son of God - Not God

Jesus is the Son of God, generated by the Father before all things. He is not God, but the Son of the only true God.

"And this is life eternal, that they might know thee the only true God, and Jesus Christ, whom thou hast sent."

John 17:3

1. Jesus has a Father

Jesus always referred to the Father as "my God" and "my Father". God has no God above Him.

2. Jesus was Sent

The Bible says Jesus was "sent" by the Father. God is not sent by anyone, He sends.

The Holy Spirit

The Power and Presence of God - Not a Person

The Holy Spirit is the active power of God, His force, His influence. It is not a third person of a trinity, but the very presence and power of God operating.

"But this is that which was spoken by the prophet Joel; And it shall come to pass in the last days, saith God, I will pour out of my Spirit upon all flesh..."

Acts 2:16-17

Bible vs Trinity

The Bible Says The Trinity Doctrine Says
One God - the Father (1 Cor 8:6) One God in three persons
Jesus is the Son of God (Mat 16:16) Jesus is "God the Son"
The Spirit is of God (Mat 10:20) The Spirit is "God the Holy Spirit"

Serious Reflection: Is it Idolatry?

"If you worship a 'god' that does not exist in the Bible, are you not worshipping an idol? If the Trinity is not in the Bible, worshipping a 'triune god' is worshipping an image created by men."

God is looking for true worshippers who worship Him in spirit and in truth.
