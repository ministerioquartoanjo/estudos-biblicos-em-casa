Study Comments

Jesus came to this Earth to teach us how to live righteously, obeying the Father's Ten Commandments, and to die to pay the debt for our sins. Then, He rose again and went to heaven to continue working for the salvation of our souls. He was consecrated by God as High Priest to minister on our behalf.

ðŸ“– Read: Hebrews 8:1, 2; Hebrews 5:5-10

His ministry consists of the work of intercession for us and in us. He intercedes in our hearts, inviting us to repentance. Then, when we confess our sins, He intercedes with God and obtains for us forgiveness and purification. Purification involves giving us power to obey and not repeat the same error.

ðŸ“– Read: Romans 8:26, 34; 1 John 1:9

The work of Christ's ministry as Priest in the heavenly sanctuary was prefigured by the ordinances of the sanctuary given by God to Moses. Therefore, we can understand the work of Christ as Priest in greater depth from the study of the Hebrew sanctuary.

ðŸ“– Read: Hebrews 8:5; Hebrews 9:1-5

Jesus Christ is the only intercessor consecrated by God to act on our behalf. The only one through whom we obtain any blessing from heaven. This work of intercession continued every day, accumulating the record of sins that needed to be purified.

ðŸ“– Read: 1 Timothy 2:5; Colossians 3:17; Hebrews 9:6

The Final Atonement

Just as in the earthly sanctuary there was a special day of purification (Day of Atonement), Christ's final work before returning to Earth will be to perform the atonement - the cleansing of the heavenly sanctuary. Once completed, He will return to Earth to seek His faithful and obedient people.

ðŸ“– Read: Leviticus 16:29, 30, 32-34
