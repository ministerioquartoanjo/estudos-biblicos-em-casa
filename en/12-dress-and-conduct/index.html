Biblical Foundations

This study explores the Apostle Paul's guidance on clothing and behavior in church, based primarily on 1 Timothy 2:9-12 and 1 Corinthians 11:2-16.

Paul emphasizes the importance of modesty, decency, and common sense as a reflection of a transformed heart.

"Whose adorning let it not be that outward... but let it be the hidden man of the heart"

1 Peter 3:3-4

Core Principles

  • Glorify God in everything
  • Respect the creational order
  • Edify the body of Christ
  • Witness with holiness

Guidance for Women

Discreet and Decent Clothing

Recommended Characteristics:
  • Clothes that cover adequately
  • Sober colors and patterns
  • Simplicity and elegance

"In like manner also, that women adorn themselves in modest apparel, with shamefacedness and sobriety..."

1 Timothy 2:9

Long Hair - Woman's Glory

Paul teaches that long hair is a woman's natural glory, given by God as a natural covering.

  • Symbol of femininity
  • Natural covering given by God
  • Creational distinction

"But if a woman have long hair, it is a glory to her: for her hair is given her for a covering."

1 Corinthians 11:15

Veil when Praying (Married Women)

For married women, the use of a veil during prayer represents:

Submission

To the husband as head

Reverence

Before God in prayer

Order

Established creational plan

Order of Honor Established by God

Paul establishes the creational order of authority and submission based on the divine plan:

GOD THE FATHER
JESUS CHRIST
MAN
WOMAN
Meaning of the Order:
  • God the Father: Supreme authority over all creation
  • Jesus Christ: Submissive to the Father, head of the Church
  • Man: Head of the woman, image of God
  • Woman: Glory of the man, suitable helper
Important Principles:
  • Order does not mean inferiority
  • All have equal value in Christ
  • Different roles, equal dignity
  • Reflection of the trinitarian order

"But I would have you know, that the head of every man is Christ; and the head of the woman is the man; and the head of Christ is God."

1 Corinthians 11:3

Quietness and Learning

Paul orientates that women should learn in quietness and submissiveness during public worship.

  • Doctrinal quietness in public worship
  • Instruct their own children
  • Ministry among women

Guidance for Men

Decent and Respectful Clothing

Men should dress in a respectful and dignified manner, reflecting their position of spiritual leadership.

  • Clean and well-maintained clothes
  • Suitable for the sacred environment
  • Honoring God and the position

"Whether therefore ye eat, or drink, or whatsoever ye do, do all to the glory of God."

1 Corinthians 10:31

Clean and Short Hair

Paul teaches that it is a shame for a man to have long hair, maintaining the creational distinction between man and woman.

  • Preferably short
  • Always well-combed
  • Clean and tidy

"Doth not even nature itself teach you, that, if a man have long hair, it is a shame unto him?"

1 Corinthians 11:14

Head Uncovered when Praying

Men should pray and prophesy with their head uncovered, demonstrating their position as the image and glory of God.

Image of God

Reflection of divine glory

Leadership

Head of the woman

Reverence

Direct access to God
