The Cleansing of the Sanctuary

Daniel 8:14 presents the longest time prophecy in the Bible. To understand it, we need to look at the **Earthly Sanctuary**, which was a "model" of the heavenly one. The Day of Atonement (Yom Kippur) was the day when the sanctuary was cleaned of the records of sins accumulated during the year.

The Day-Year Principle

In symbolic prophecies, God uses a day to represent a year. This principle is found in Numbers 14:34 and Ezekiel 4:6. Therefore, 2300 evenings and mornings are 2300 literal years.

The Starting Point

Daniel 9:25 gives the start date: "from the going forth of the commandment to restore and to build Jerusalem". The final and complete decree was given by Artaxerxes in 457 B.C.

Key Points

Prophetic Year

Understanding that 2300 evenings and mornings equal 2300 literal years is the key to unlocking this prophecy (Numbers 14:34).

The End of the 70 Weeks

The time granted especially to the Jewish people ended in 34 A.D., after which the gospel went to the whole world.

Perfect Fulfillment

The 70 weeks (490 years) were fulfilled exactly, confirming that the 2300 years ended in 1844.

Common Questions

What happened on October 22, 1844? ▼

Jesus did not return to Earth, but moved to a new phase in His heavenly ministry. He entered the Most Holy Place of the Heavenly Sanctuary to begin the Investigative Judgment (the cleansing of the sanctuary), the final step before His coming.

Why did Daniel 8:27 say he was sick and did not understand? ▼

Daniel was overwhelmed by the vision of the 2300 days because he thought the captivity of his people (which should last 70 years according to Jeremiah) would now last 2300 years. He only understood later, in chapter 9, when Gabriel explained the starting point and the cutting off of the 70 weeks for his people.

What does "evenings and mornings" mean? ▼

This is the biblical way of counting a full day (Genesis 1). In the sanctuary context, it also refers to the morning and evening sacrifices. Therefore, 2300 evenings and mornings are 2300 prophetic days (years).
