The Essence of Babylon's Fall

The fall of Babylon is not a physical event of immediate destruction, but a spiritual and moral fall. It occurs when religious institutions abandon the "Truth" (the Word of God) and begin to intoxicate nations with the "Wine" of their false doctrines.

The Wine of Babylon

Represents false doctrines that confuse human understanding. Just as alcohol numbs the physical senses, these doctrines prevent people from discerning the will of God.

Religious Confusion

The word "Babylon" derives from "Babel", which means "Confusion". It represents a fragmented religious system that mixes the sacred with the profane and the Bible with human tradition.
